Privacy Policy for SetuNotes

Last Updated: 17th July 2025

SetuNotes ("the App") is a free note-taking application that provides access to NCERT textbooks and competitive exam resources. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data and content in compliance with privacy laws and fair use principles.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No Authentication Required: Users can access all features without signing in.
  • No Personal Data Collected: We do not collect names, emails, or device identifiers.
  • No User Tracking: Notes/content remain on the device and are not uploaded to servers.

2. NCERT & Competitive Exam Materials

  • Source of Content:
    • All NCERT books and public domain exam papers (e.g., UPSC prelims, JEE previous years) are sourced from official government websites (e.g., ncert.nic.in) or other freely available public repositories.
    • We do not host pirated or paywalled content.
  • Fair Use Compliance:
    • Content is provided for educational purposes only, aligning with India’s Fair Use provisions under Section 52(1)(i) of the Copyright Act, 1957.
    • The App does not modify or claim ownership of any copyrighted material.

3. Data Storage & Permissions

  • Notes: Saved locally on the user’s device; we cannot access them.
  • Permissions:
    • Storage: Used only to save/export notes or access downloaded PDFs.
    • Internet: Required for Admob ads and fetching public-domain resources.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Admob: Shows non-personalized ads (Google’s Privacy Policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy).
  • Analytics: Basic crash reports (anonymous) via Firebase.

5. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted in the App’s "About" section. Continued use implies acceptance.
