Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how our app works

Last Updated: August 20, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Roxy AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play Store.

Key Point: Our app is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require user registration, do not collect personal data, and do not store your information permanently.

By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use our application.

Information We Don't Collect

We are committed to your privacy. Our app does not collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact details
  • Account Information: No user accounts, login credentials, or profiles
  • Location Data: No GPS coordinates or location tracking
  • Device Information: No device IDs, hardware specifications, or system details
  • Usage Analytics: No tracking of app usage patterns or user behavior
  • Content Storage: No permanent storage of your text inputs or generated audio files

No Registration Required: Our app works without requiring any form of user registration, sign-up, or account creation.

How Our Services Work

On-Device Text-to-Speech

  • Processing happens entirely on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Generated audio files remain on your device
  • Complete offline functionality

Cloud Text-to-Speech

  • Text input is sent securely to our cloud TTS service
  • Data is transmitted using encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Our service processes the text and returns audio data
  • Your text is automatically deleted after processing

Important: For Cloud TTS, your text is temporarily sent to third-party cloud services for processing. The text is processed securely and deleted immediately after audio generation is complete.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob to support development and keep the app free. AdMob may collect certain information including:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information for ad targeting
  • App usage data related to ad interaction

This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Cloud TTS Service

When using Cloud TTS features, your text is processed by third-party cloud text-to-speech services. These services process your text securely and delete it immediately after audio generation.

Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads).

Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your privacy:

  • Encrypted Transmission: All cloud communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No Data Storage: We do not store any user data on our servers
  • Temporary Processing: Cloud data is processed and immediately deleted
  • Local Storage: On-device processing keeps all data on your device
  • Minimal Permissions: App requests only essential permissions for functionality

Privacy by Design: Our app architecture is designed to minimize data collection and maximize user privacy from the ground up.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional data rights don't apply. However, you have:

  • Right to Uninstall: Remove the app at any time
  • Right to Opt-Out: Disable personalized ads through device settings
  • Right to Transparency: Full disclosure of how the app works
  • Right to Contact: Reach out with any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect personal information, there are no special considerations for children under 13. However:

  • AdMob ads are filtered to be family-friendly
  • No account creation or personal data entry is required
  • Parents can disable personalized ads through device settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date
  • Announced through app update notes if significant
  • Effective immediately upon posting

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
