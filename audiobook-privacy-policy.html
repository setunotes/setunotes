Section 01

Overview

Welcome to UPSC AudioBook ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Android application. We are committed to handling your data responsibly in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

By installing or using our application, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application immediately.

Quick Summary: We collect only the data necessary to provide you with UPSC study content — including NCERT audiobooks and important UPSC reference materials. PDF reading is always free. An active subscription is required only to access audio content. Your payment details are handled entirely by Razorpay and never stored on our servers.

Section 03

Authentication & Account Security

We offer two methods of authentication, both integrated with Firebase Authentication and verified against our own backend server:

Login Method Data Accessed Stored By Us Verification
Email & Password Email address, hashed password Email, UID (Firebase) Firebase + our backend
Google Sign-In Name, email, profile photo, Google ID token Email, display name, photo URL, UID Google OAuth + Firebase + our backend
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — Firebase handles all password hashing and security.
  • Google Sign-In tokens are validated server-side against Google's OAuth2 endpoints.
  • Our backend issues session tokens to verify subscription status and access control independently.
  • You can delete your account at any time from Settings, which will erase all associated data within 30 days.
Section 04

Payments & Subscription

Audio content in the app requires an active subscription. Payments are processed exclusively through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We do not store, process, or have access to your credit/debit card details, UPI VPA, or net banking credentials.

Free Access: Browsing and reading PDF versions of all UPSC content — NCERT books and important reference materials — is completely free and does not require a subscription or login.
Feature Free Subscription Required
PDF Reading (NCERT & UPSC Books) ✅ Always Free —
Audio Playback (All Books) ❌ ✅ Active Subscription
Download for Offline Audio ❌ ✅ Active Subscription
Bookmarks & Progress Sync ✅ (PDF only) ✅ (Audio + PDF)

Data we receive from Razorpay and store on our backend includes:

  • Razorpay Order ID and Payment ID (for verification only)
  • Subscription plan, start date, and expiry date
  • Payment status (success/failed/refunded)
  • Amount and currency — no card/banking details

Razorpay's privacy policy is available at razorpay.com/privacy

Section 05

Content & Library

Our app provides the following UPSC-specific content, all hosted on our servers:

  • NCERT Books (PDF + Audio): Standard NCERT textbooks relevant to UPSC CSE preparation (Classes 6–12 across History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science, etc.).
  • Important UPSC Reference Books (PDF + Audio): Curated list of standard references like Laxmikanth (Polity), Bipin Chandra (History), Ramesh Singh (Economy), and others.
  • Content access logs (which books you opened and how long you listened) are stored to support progress tracking and improve recommendations.
  • We do not share your reading or listening history with third parties for advertising.

Section 08

Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2+.
  • User credentials are managed exclusively by Firebase Authentication — we do not store raw passwords.
  • Our backend APIs require valid authentication tokens for all protected endpoints.
  • Payment data is stored only in Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.
  • We perform regular security reviews of our backend systems.
  • Access to user data within our team is restricted on a need-to-know basis.
Data Retention: Account data is retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, personal data is purged within 30 days. Transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and financial compliance.

Section 10

Children's Privacy

UPSC AudioBook is designed for individuals preparing for UPSC Civil Services Examinations and is intended for users aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will promptly delete that information. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal data should contact us immediately.
