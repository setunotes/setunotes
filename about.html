About SetupNotes

Your trusted provider of official NCERT books since 2024

Our Mission

SetupNotes is dedicated to making quality education accessible to every student. We believe that financial constraints should never be a barrier to learning. Our platform provides free access to all NCERT textbooks for Classes 1-12, helping millions of students achieve their academic goals.

Why We Started

Education is the foundation of society. NCERT books are the most trusted study material for CBSE students across India. We created SetupNotes to:

  • Provide free, unlimited access to NCERT books
  • Save students time searching for reliable resources
  • Create a platform optimized for fast downloads
  • Support students in their academic journey
  • Promote digital literacy and resource sharing
